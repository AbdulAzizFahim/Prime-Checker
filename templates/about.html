SIEVE ALGORITHM

Data Set Information:

The following example illustrates how the Sieve of Eratosthenes, can be used to find all the prime numbers that are less than 10000.

STEPS:

  1. Write the numbers 1 to 10000
  2. Cross out 1 because 1 is not a prime.
  3. Circle x (Here x = 3) and cross out all multiples of x. (3, 6, 9, 12, 15, ...)
  4. increment x = x + 2 until x is less than of equal 100
  5. The uncrossed numbers are the prime numbers
